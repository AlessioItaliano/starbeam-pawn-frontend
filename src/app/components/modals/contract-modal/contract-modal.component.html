Default: If the client fails to repay the loan and reclaim their pawned item(s) by the specified due date, the item(s) will be considered in default and ownership will transfer to the pawn shop. Interest Rates: The interest rates and fees applicable to the pawn loan are clearly outlined in the agreement. These rates are subject to change based on state regulations and the terms of the agreement. Redemption: Clients have the right to redeem their pawned items by paying the total loan amount, including any interest and fees, on or before the due date specified in the pawn agreement. Storage Fees: If a client requests an extension on their loan period, additional storage fees may apply. These fees will be calculated based on the duration of the extension and are payable upon item redemption. Identification: Clients must provide valid identification when pawning items.
